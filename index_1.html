About Us

Bunya Secondary School is a leading educational institution located in Mayuge District, Uganda. We offer both O-Level and A-Level programs, with a focus on academic excellence, character development, and community engagement.

Experienced Teachers

Our teachers are highly qualified and dedicated to nurturing the potential of every student.

Modern Facilities

We provide state-of-the-art classrooms, safe accommodations, and clean drinking water.

Holistic Education

We focus on academics, sports, and extracurricular activities to develop well-rounded individuals.

Academics

We offer a comprehensive curriculum for both O-Level and A-Level students, designed to prepare them for success in higher education and beyond.

O-Level Program

Our O-Level program covers a wide range of subjects, including Mathematics, Sciences, Humanities, and Languages.

A-Level Program

Our A-Level program offers specialized tracks in Sciences, Arts, and Commerce, tailored to students' career aspirations.
